This pack contains a collection of realistic 3D worms featuring different variations.Pack comes with a customizable Glowing shader
and PBR-textured Worms for high-quality visual detail and customization.Perfect for Open World, Survival, and 
Fantasy-themed games, or any other genre. All assets are optimized and ready for commercial use in your projects.

You can customize the Glowing Worms materials by adjusting parameters such as Frozen color and Power.
Glowing worm materials created with Unity's shadergraph.And the asset uses Unity's standard material for PBR textured variation.

Note: This is the free version of our asset Frozen Winter Worms.

Technical Details:-
		- Material(s) Used: Unity standard material, Custom Material (Made with Shadergraph) for Glowing shader.
		- Model(s) : Small worms, Fat worms
		- Animations count : 1 (Look animation)

		- Poly count (in Tris) :  Small worms (736),  Fat worm 1(2376)
		- Unwrapped : YES
		- Prefab Variations : 16 (8 Small Worm, 8 Fat Worm)
		- Texture Size : 512 x 512
		- URP support : YES
		- PBR Textures (Albedo, Metallic Roughness, Normal)
